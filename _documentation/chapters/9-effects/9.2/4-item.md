### COMPRESSOR
Compresses the loudness of a signal to make it more uniform in loudness. Can also modify transients.
MAKEUP: on/off
KNEE/GRAPH: switch between knee indicator and spectrogram
THRESHOLD: -42–0.00 dB
RATIO: 1.0–100
ATTACK: 0.010–30 ms
RELEASE: 10.0–1200 ms
