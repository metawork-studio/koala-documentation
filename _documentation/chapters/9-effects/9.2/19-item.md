### RING MOD
Ring modulator.

* FREQ: 1.0 Hz to 8.00 kHz
* MIX: 0.0–100%
* DRIVE: 0.0–18.0 dB
* LFO: red dot pulsates in set speed and depth
* DEPTH: 0.0–100%
* SPEED: 0.000000–30 Hz / 1/64–8 bars
* STEREO: 0.00–100%
* WAVEFORM: Sinus, Square, Triangle, Saw, Inverted Saw, Random Square, Random Triangle
* TEMPO SYNC: on/off
